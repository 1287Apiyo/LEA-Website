Empower young minds through Technology and Education

At LEA Foundation, we provide hands-on coding and technology training, support personal and professional growth, foster innovation, and create equal opportunities for all. Join us in our mission to transform lives and build a brighter future.

Get inspired

At LEA Foundation, we inspire and empower kids aged 9 to 18 by immersing them in the world of technology. Through hands-on training sessions, we provide valuable learning experiences for those who might otherwise miss out.

Our Recent Projects
Creating Exposure

Empowering kids through hands-on coding bootcamps and digital literacy workshops, focusing on underprivileged communities.

Empower Young Minds

At Mukuru Skills Training Centre, we introduced the basics of computers to eager learners, many of whom had never used one before. Our hands-on workshops covered essential skills, from understanding hardware to navigating software, opening doors to new opportunities and inspiring a passion for technology.

Fund Laptop Purchase

Currently, our tutors provide the laptops for these sessions. We are actively seeking funding to equip our learners with their own laptops, ensuring they have continuous access to the tools they need to grow and learn..

Programming

We’ve recently introduced website building to our learners, and successfully completed our very first website project together. This hands-on experience sparked greater interest and excitement among the students, as they explored the world of web development and created something tangible with their newfound skills..
